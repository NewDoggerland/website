About New Doggerland

New Doggerland is a long-horizon land project centered on dogs, people, and place. It is being developed as a permanent estate where rescue, work, rest, recreation, and shared life are intentionally held together rather than separated into programs.

The project proceeds with restraint: land first, systems second, growth only when it can be carried without loss of dignity for humans or animals. New Doggerland is not designed to scale quickly, but to endure.

The project is governed by explicit ethical constraints, public accountability, and operational transparency. Decisions are documented, funds are traceable, and practices are structured to withstand scrutiny over time rather than rely on trust or goodwill.

New Doggerland is in a pre-registration phase. We are not yet a registered charitable organization and are not currently soliciting tax-deductible donations. Our focus at this stage is governance, compliance, and readiness.
